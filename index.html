The Global Game Jam (GGJ) is the world's largest game jam event. It is the growth of an idea that in today’s heavily connected world, we could come together, be creative, share experiences and express ourselves in a multitude of ways using video games – it is very universal. Volta is Hosting a Halifax Jam Site on January 23-25th

Teach Me How to is a series of events run by the SMU Computer Science and Mathematics Society where students and guest speakers present a topic they know with the goal of growing the community. In preparation for Global Game Jam we invite you to two events to learn how easy it is to make games. Follow along or watch. You can make games!

When

2D - January 21 - 7:30 pm
3D - January 22 - 7:30 pm

Where

Saint Mary's University Loyola Building - LA 176

How

If you are interested in following along we recommend you download Unity on your laptop in preparation. It is one of the most popular game engines in the world and has an impressive list of games that it powers.
